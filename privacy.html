Privacy Policy

Skunked: Way of the Spray — Last updated: February 10, 2026

Summary

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Skunked: Way of the Spray is a free browser-based game that runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, and no tracking.

What Data We Collect

None. The game does not collect any personal information, usage data, or device identifiers.

Local Storage

The game uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • High scores
  • Achievement progress
  • Display and performance preferences (e.g., mobile performance mode)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Cookies

We do not use cookies.

Third-Party Services

The game does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If the game is hosted on a platform such as Netlify or Vercel, those providers may collect standard web server logs (IP address, request URL, user agent) as part of their hosting infrastructure. We do not access or use that data. Refer to your hosting provider's privacy policy for details.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data, the game is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or any other age).

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how data is handled, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since we collect nothing, we don't anticipate changes.
